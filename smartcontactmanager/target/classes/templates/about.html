About Contact Management

Contact Management is a web application developed using Spring Boot, aimed at efficiently managing contacts. It provides features such as adding, editing, and deleting contacts, as well as searching and organizing contacts based on various criteria.

This application allows users to store contact details, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, and more. It offers a user-friendly interface, ensuring smooth navigation and ease of use.

With Contact Management, users can keep their contacts organized and accessible, making it easier to manage and communicate with their contacts effectively.
